Intelligent Automation

🎯

AI Bullshit Detector

Automatically identifies projects with repeated "in progress" updates but no tangible accomplishments. Flags stalemate situations before they become critical.

💡

Smart Briefing Generator

Auto-generates executive summaries highlighting key accomplishments, critical blockers, and resource needs—organized exactly how you want to see them.

📈

Predictive Insights

Machine learning identifies patterns and predicts "This project will likely stall in 2 weeks" based on historical data and update sentiment.

🔔

Proactive Recommendations

AI suggests specific actions for stalled projects: "Consider reassigning resources" or "Schedule stakeholder alignment meeting."

📊

Sentiment Analysis

Tracks team morale and project confidence over time. Visual mood indicators reveal when teams are struggling before they explicitly escalate.

🎤

Voice-to-Update

Mobile-friendly voice input for on-the-go updates. Speak your accomplishments; AI structures them into proper format automatically.
